Tech Lead - Cloud Native Infrastructure

We are seeking an experienced and innovative Tech Lead to join our rapidly growing team. As a Tech Lead, you will play a crucial role in designing, implementing, and maintaining our cloud-native infrastructure and distributed systems.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Lead the design and implementation of scalable, resilient microservices architectures
  • Spearhead Kubernetes-based deployments and manage complex container orchestration
  • Develop and maintain Infrastructure as Code using Terraform and other modern IaC tools
  • Optimize database performance and ensure data integrity across various database systems
  • Mentor team members and foster a culture of technical excellence and innovation
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to deliver high-quality software solutions

Required Skills and Experience:

  • 8+ years of software development experience, with 5+ years focusing on distributed systems
  • Expert-level knowledge of Kubernetes and microservices architectures
  • Strong proficiency in Go, with experience in other languages such as Python, Java, or Rust
  • Extensive experience with public cloud platforms (GCP, AWS, or Azure)
  • Deep understanding of RESTful API design and implementation
  • Proven track record of leading technical projects and mentoring team members

If you're passionate about cutting-edge technology and thrive in a fast-paced, remote-first environment, we want to hear from you. Join us in shaping the future of cloud-native infrastructure and make a significant impact in a rapidly growing company.
